Welcome to Ukraines Aid

Ukraine's Aid Help Center is a collaboration of Christian churches, community organizations, and individuals dedicated to assisting people in need.

Ukraine is Europe's second-largest country, after Russia, with a population of 44 million people. Unfortunately, children in Eastern Ukraine have spent the last eight years growing up in a conflict zone, facing gunfire, shelling, and being uprooted from their homes. Fighting in Ukraine has pushed children and families to flee to neighboring countries, with over 3 million people crossing the border thus far.
