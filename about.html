Who are we?

Ukraine's Aid Help Center is a collaboration of Christian churches, community organizations, and individuals dedicated to assisting people in need. Through its core areas of service, the organization aspires to establish peace by saving people.

Save Ukraine Help Center organized forty canteens for needy persons without access to food across the Donetsk and Luhansk regions. In addition, the organization provides food and non-food items, such as clothing, medicine, and hygiene kits, to vulnerable people living in crisis zones. Humanitarian aid is generally sent from outside the country and distributed to persons living in conflict zones.

Helping children is one of Save Ukraine Help Center's top concerns. As a result, it engages in activities such as collecting donations and locating doctors and medical facilities to treat children and give them with whatever they require in terms of physical and mental well-being.

In September, just before the start of the school year, volunteers and missionaries in the Donetsk region organized an outdoor mobile camp for children that included sports activities and music concerts in order to integrate children into the community. Backpacks were given to the most vulnerable children, which were purchased with donations.

International missionaries come to the Save Ukraine Aid Center to share their experiences and make ideas on how to help people, so assisting in the integration of IDPs and the building of peace in the Donbass.
